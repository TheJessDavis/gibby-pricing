# The Gibby · Price a Class

A pricing calculator for teachers at The Gibby. Add up your materials, plug in what you want to earn, score your class on the tier rubric, and see the 60/40 split break down live.

**Live tool:** open `index.html` in a browser, or visit the GitHub Pages URL if this repo has Pages enabled.

## What it does

- Adds up materials (with source URLs) into a total and per-student cost
- Suggests a ticket price from your target pay
- Scores your class on 5 factors (technique, prep, instruction intensity, training required, materials cost) to pick a tier and price range
- Shows the 60/40 split live, per student and per full class
- Warns when materials break the ~20% budget or the price falls outside the tier range
- Shows earnings at minimum, planned, and full enrollment so you can pressure-test the numbers before you post

## The split

- **60% of the ticket price goes to the teacher.**
- **40% stays with The Gibby** to cover space, admin, and materials.
- Materials should stay under about half of the Gibby's 40%, roughly **20% of the ticket**.

## Tiers

| Score | Tier | Range |
|---|---|---|
| 5 to 7 | Tier 1 · Intro | $25 to $45 |
| 8 to 11 | Tier 2 · Intermediate | $45 to $65 |
| 12 to 15 | Tier 3 · Advanced | $65 to $100+ |

## Hosting

It's a single static HTML file. No build step, no dependencies. Host on GitHub Pages, drop it on any static host, or open the file locally.
